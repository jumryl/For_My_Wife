Just like our journey, this bouquet holds meaning beyond words: a reminder that even in a short time, you’ve already become someone truly special to me.

For our second monthsary, I wanted to give you something that reflected what my heart feels, something bright, meaningful, and full of love. That’s why I chose a bouquet made of sunflowers and tulips. The sunflowers remind me so much of you, radiating warmth, positivity, and a kind of loyalty that lights up every moment we share. The tulips, on the other hand, speak the words my heart has been quietly saying all along: a deep love, sincere and growing, and a promise of forever with you.

This bouquet may seem simple, but it holds more meaning than I can express. Every flower represents a part of this journey we’ve started, beautiful, delicate, and real. In just one month, you’ve already become someone incredibly special to me. You’ve brought happiness into my life in a way I didn’t expect but always hoped for. Your presence, your laughter, and the way you make me feel safe and loved, it’s something I’ll never take for granted.

I know it’s still early in our journey, but if these first 30 days are anything to go by, I know we have something truly worth holding on to. Thank you for being part of my life, for accepting this small gesture, and for showing me a kind of love that feels pure and genuine. This is only the beginning, and I can’t wait to see where our story takes us next—side by side, heart to heart.
