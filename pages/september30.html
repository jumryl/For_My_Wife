From Baptisms to Big Dreams
; Our 9th Monthsary Adventure

Hello wifey, happy 9th monthsary! 🥰 Another month, another event, this time it’s your friend’s baby’s baptism. From weddings to baptisms… parang complete na ang “journey of life” checklist natin ah! Next year na ba tayo? Hahaha! 😆

Grabe, every time may ganitong event, hindi ko maiwasang maging excited at happy. Seeing these milestones, weddings, baptisms, makes me imagine our future together. Sabi nila, sa una lang daw sweet. Pero bakit habang tumatagal, mas lalo akong na-iinlove sayo? ❤️

Oo, minsan nagkaka-initan tayo. Minsan you treat me like kaaway instead of your hubby. Pero tandaan mo, treat me like your husband na “bunal-bunalan” mo, pero in a funny, loving way ha. Hahahah! Kahit ganyan, I still choose you every day. Always. 🥰

Happy 9th monthsary, my love. Thank you for being with me through the sweet moments and the small tampo moments. I’ll keep making you laugh (kahit corny jokes), I’ll keep being patient (kahit dragon-mode ka 😆), and I’ll keep loving you more and more as time goes by.

Still yours, always,
Jumryl
