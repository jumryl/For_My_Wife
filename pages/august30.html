August 30, 2025

Our First Wedding Together
; A Day to Remember

Hello wifey, happy 8th monthsary! 🥰 This month was not your usual “flowers and pizza” type of celebration. Nope. This month, I got the full *dragon edition* of you. 🐉🔥 Hahaha!

Imagine this: we’re at your friend’s brother’s wedding, my first time ever attending a full wedding, from the church ceremony all the way to the reception. I was there, all starry-eyed, feeling like I was in a movie… and then there’s you. Beautiful. Stunning. Radiant. And also… very maldita. 😅 Because who else would *walk away without me* just because I had no umbrella? Hahaha! There I was, like a telenovela extra, waiting in the rain until 8:00 pm, hoping the main character (you) would turn back dramatically and say, "Hubs, ali na lakaw nata!” But no, the dragon queen didn’t even look back. 😂

And yet… I wasn’t angry. Not even a bit. Because even when you’re the full dragon version of yourself, even when your patience is shorter than my hair after a bad barber cut, I still choose you. Always. ❤️ I know I can be “sungogan” at times, trying to make you laugh with jokes that aren’t even funny (admit it, sometimes you laugh just out of pity 😆). But that’s me. That’s how much I love seeing you smile, even when you’re tampo. Even when you forgot it was our monthsary because we were both so busy. (Don’t forget next time ha, hahah!)

We’ve had small fights. Misunderstandings. Unspoken frustrations. You, quiet. Me, overthinking. Me, cracking bad jokes to lighten the mood. You, rolling your eyes. But still, we end up right here, together. That’s our thing. That’s love. Not the perfect Instagram kind, but the kind where you walk away sometimes, and I still wait for you. The kind where even if you turn into a dragon, I’ll still hold the umbrella for you next time. 😂☔. And yes, I still laughed at myself that day, waiting, soaking, but smiling, because somehow, even your “dragon” moments are part of what makes us *us.*

Here’s to more months. More little battles. More sweet recoveries. More future plans. And more of me waiting for you, even when you’re walking ahead like a queen. I love you, not just when you’re sweet and smiling, but also when you’re moody, sarcastic, or full-on dragon-mode. Even when you forget our monthsary. Even when my jokes don’t land. Always, always you. 🥰

Still yours, always,
Jumryl
