Even Storms
Can't Stop Us

My Baby, My Calm, My Wifey

Happy 7th Monthsary, Wifey!

We made it through another month, seven months of love, laughter, late, night kulitan, and yes… a few *mini dramas* over things like “where to eat” or “bakit nag bago ka?” 😅

This month tested us in different ways. We had little arguments, moments of misunderstanding, and changes we both felt. Our real selves started to show more, and I think that’s a good thing. It means we’re becoming more honest, more real, more... *us*.

But even through the tampuhan, the mood swings (yes, pati ako!), the unspoken expectations, and the times we just stared at the wall instead of talking, we still chose to fix things. We still held on. That’s love, right? Not always perfect, but always worth it.

So please, my love, let’s promise again: no more “break-up” threats, kahit joke lang. Kasi kahit biro, medyo nakakakaba ‘yun. Remember, we promised “happily ever after,” not “happily hanggang magkalituhan.” 😂

Let’s not forget the good times either, the laughs, the food trips, the late-night lambingan, and the random moments where we just knew, “Ikaw nga.” Let’s keep working on ourselves, for each other, and for our future. Whether it’s a dream house or just figuring out who does the dishes tonight (spoiler: ako na if may lambing 😁).

We’re growing, not just in love, but in patience, in trust, and in our ability to bounce back stronger after every storm. And even if life throws more plot twists at us, let’s be the team that survives the whole season, no cancellation, no recast. 💪

I love you until the end of time. Even when we argue. Even when I’m makulit. Even when you’re quiet and I have to guess kung galit ka ba or gutom lang. 😅

To forever with you, no edits, no cuts, just us, learning and loving day by day.

Yours forever,

Hubs
